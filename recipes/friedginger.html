Chha Knhei Sach Mone

This spicy main dish, cha knhei sach moan in Cambodian, made by chef Andrea Nguyen, based on the original Khmer recipe from Rotanak Ros (Chef Nak)'s cookbook Nhum (Rotanak Food Media, 2019).

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Cremini Mushroom
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Palm Sugar
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sea Salt
  • Neutral Oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken
  2. Prepare the mushroom, Garlic and Ginger
  3. Add sugar, soy souce, oyster sauce
  4. Prepare the pan and oil
  5. Stir fried
  6. Serve and Enjoy
