Fish Amok

Fish Amok is a traditional Cambodian dish that has gained widespread popularity both within the country and internationally. It's a flavorful and aromatic curry made with fresh fish, coconut milk, and a variety of spices.

  • Tumeric
  • Lemongrass
  • Galangal
  • Lime leaves
  • Garlic
  • Chili peppers
  • Shrimp Paste
  • Fish sauce
  • Lime juice
  • Sugar
  • Banana leaves
  • Water
  1. Marinate the fish
  2. Prepare the curry
  3. Add the fish
  4. Steam in banana leaves
  5. Server and enjoy
