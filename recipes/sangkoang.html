Sangko Ang

Sachko Ang is a popular Cambodian street food that showcases the country's vibrant and flavorful cuisine. It's essentially a grilled beef skewer, marinated in a savory blend of spices and herbs, and served on a piece of baguette.

Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Fish Sauce
  • Lime Juice
  • Garlic
  • Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Chili Flakes

Steps

  1. Marinate the beef
  2. Prepare the skewers
  3. Thread the beef
  4. Grill the beef
  5. Assemble the Sachko Ang
  6. Serve and Enjoy
